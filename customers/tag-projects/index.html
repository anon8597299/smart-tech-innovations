Materials

Steel. Aluminium. Stainless.
We Work Across All Three.

Every material has its place. We'll advise which suits your project for strength, finish, cost, and long-term performance.

Mild Steel

The workhorse. Strong, weldable, and cost-effective for structural applications, frames, and fit-out components that will be powder coated or painted. Ideal for most commercial and industrial projects.

Aluminium

Lightweight and corrosion-resistant. Preferred for screens, balustrades, architectural trims, and exterior applications where weight and weather resistance matter. Anodised or powder coat finish available.

Stainless Steel

Grade 304 and 316 available. The standard for healthcare, commercial kitchens, marine environments, and architectural applications where hygiene, durability, and appearance are non-negotiable.

What we do

Complete Solar Solutions
for Homes & Businesses

From solar power and hot water to pool heating and ventilation — one trusted local team for everything.

Solar Power Systems

Grid-connected solar panels using only premium brands — REC panels, Fronius and SolarEdge inverters. Sized right for your home or business.

Solar Hot Water

Cut your hot water bills by up to 80%. We install Rinnai, Sanden and Solar Ark systems — the most reliable brands on the market.

Pool Heating

Extend your swimming season year-round with Sunbather and Hayward solar pool heating — efficient, durable and designed for the Australian climate.

Ventilation Systems

Solarwhiz solar roof ventilation removes heat and moisture from your roof cavity — reducing cooling costs and protecting your home year-round.

Pool Accessories

Filters, pumps, cleaners and chlorinators from leading brands. We supply and install everything your pool needs to run cleanly and efficiently.

Gate Controllers

Solar-powered gate automation for homes and rural properties. No mains connection required — fully self-sufficient and reliable year-round.

Logo Usage Rules

  • Minimum size: 120px wide (horizontal logo)
  • Always use SVG for web, PNG for print/email
  • Never stretch or distort the logo
  • Never change the logo colours
  • Always use white version on dark backgrounds
  • Maintain clear space of at least the icon height on all sides
  • Google Business Profile: use logo-square.svg (400×400)
  • Social media header: use the gradient background

Where to Use Each Asset

Google Business Profile
  • Profile photo: logo-square.svg (400×400)
  • Cover photo: gradient background + white logo
  • Post images: white logo on indigo background
Social Media
  • Profile picture: logo-square.svg

📊 The learning period

The first 14 days: Google trains. We watch closely.

Google Ads needs approximately 14 days to collect data and train its algorithm before campaigns perform at their best. This is a Google platform reality — Smart Bidding, Quality Score, and conversion tracking all require historical signal before they can optimise effectively.

During the first 14 days, we observe rather than intervene — pausing keywords before Google has enough data can permanently damage campaign learning. From day 15 onward, full automated optimisation switches on.

1–14

Observation period

Daily reports land every morning. KPI alerts shown for reference only. Auto-pause disabled to protect algorithm training. Budget pacing alerts always actionable.

15+

Full optimisation active

Auto-pause fires on dead keywords. KPI assessment raises live alerts. All seven monitoring tasks run at full sensitivity. Campaigns start paying for themselves.

Do you offer personal styling sessions?
Yes, and they're completely free. Our sessions run 30–45 minutes and are perfect if you have a special event, want to refresh your wardrobe, or just want honest advice on what suits you. Book by calling (02) 6361 4422 — sessions are popular so book at least a week ahead.
Can I return or exchange items?
Yes. We offer a 30-day returns and exchange policy on full-price items. Items must be unworn, unwashed and in original condition with tags attached. Return in-store or via post. Sale items are exchange-only.
Do you ship to Sydney, Melbourne and other cities?
Yes — we ship Australia-wide. Orders over $150 ship free. Standard shipping is $10 for orders under $150. Metro orders typically arrive within 2–3 business days. You'll receive tracking via Australia Post.
How often does new stock arrive?
New arrivals hit the floor every fortnight, with additional drops mid-season. Follow us on Instagram at @theloftboutique — we post every new delivery as it arrives.

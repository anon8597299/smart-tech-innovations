Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to what we hear most often.

Individual tax returns start from $220 for straightforward PAYG employees, and go up based on complexity — investment properties, capital gains, sole trader income and other schedules each add to the scope. During your free 20-minute consultation we will give you a fixed-fee quote before any work begins, so there are no surprises at invoice time.

For most individuals: your PAYG payment summary (or ATO pre-fill), bank statements for interest, dividend statements, receipts for work-related expenses, and details of any investment property income and expenses. Sole traders should also bring a profit and loss summary or bank statements covering the full financial year. We will send you a personalised checklist after you book so you arrive fully prepared.

Yes — SMSF administration is one of our core services. We handle everything from establishment and trust deed preparation through to annual financial statements, tax returns, audit coordination and investment strategy compliance. Sarah Chen holds specialist SMSF accreditation through CPA Australia. We currently administer SMSFs for clients across Bathurst, Orange and remotely throughout NSW.

A registered tax agent is licensed by the Tax Practitioners Board (TPB) to prepare and lodge tax returns on your behalf and provide tax advice for a fee — all our principals are registered tax agents. A CPA accountant holds additional qualifications through CPA Australia covering financial reporting, business advisory, management accounting and more. At Clearview, you get both: registered tax agents who are also CPAs, meaning you receive tax compliance and broader business advice under one roof.

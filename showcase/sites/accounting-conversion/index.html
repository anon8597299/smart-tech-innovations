Questions

Frequently Asked

When should I lodge my tax return?

Individual returns are due by 31 October each year if lodging yourself. Clients who lodge through a registered tax agent like Clearview receive an automatic extension, often to May the following year. We recommend lodging as early as possible to receive any refund faster.

What records do I need to bring?

For individual returns: payment summaries or income statements from myGov, bank interest statements, any dividend or rental income records, and receipts for work-related expenses. We send you a simple checklist when you book. Many documents can be retrieved via our ATO portal access with your consent.

Can I switch to Clearview mid-year?

Absolutely. We handle the transition from your previous accountant, including obtaining prior year records and picking up mid-year bookkeeping. There is no disruption to your compliance obligations. Most clients are fully transitioned within two weeks.

Do you offer a Xero setup service?

Yes. As a Xero Gold Partner, we set up and configure Xero for new clients, migrate data from MYOB or other software, train your staff and provide ongoing support. A proper Xero setup pays for itself quickly in time savings and improved visibility over your numbers.

What We Do

Six Core Service Areas

Whether you need a clear direction, a performance turnaround or a structured exit, Apex provides the independent expertise to make it happen.

01

Strategic Planning

Facilitated planning sessions that produce a clear, actionable 3 to 5 year roadmap. We align your leadership team and convert goals into quarterly milestones you can track.

02

Business Performance Review

A rigorous diagnostic of your operations, financials, people and market position. We identify where value is being lost and map out a prioritised improvement plan.

03

Exit and Succession Planning

Structured planning to maximise your business value ahead of a sale, management buyout or generational transfer. We prepare the business, the numbers and the story.

04

Financial Modelling

Board-ready financial models for capital raising, acquisitions, scenario planning and budget forecasting. We turn your numbers into a compelling picture for stakeholders and lenders.

05

Marketing Strategy

Market positioning, competitive differentiation and go-to-market planning. We help you own a clear position in your market and build the systems to grow revenue with intention.

06

Leadership Coaching

One-on-one executive coaching for business owners and senior leaders navigating growth, transition or complexity. Practical, direct and confidential coaching grounded in real-world experience.

Questions

Frequently Asked

What size businesses do you work with?

Most Apex clients turn over between $1M and $20M per year across a wide range of industries. We work particularly well with privately-owned businesses where the owner is directly involved. Smaller businesses can access our Leadership Coaching service as a cost-effective entry point.

How do engagements typically work?

Most engagements begin with a 3-month diagnostic and strategy phase, followed by an ongoing retainer for implementation support. Some clients engage for specific one-off projects like exit planning or financial modelling. All engagements begin with a free 30-minute discovery call.

Is my information kept confidential?

Completely. Every Apex engagement is bound by a formal confidentiality agreement before any commercial information is shared. We do not discuss client situations with third parties and our case studies are always anonymised with client approval.

Do you work outside of Orange?

Yes. David regularly works with clients in Bathurst, Dubbo, Parkes, Forbes, Mudgee and across Central West NSW. Approximately 40% of our work is conducted remotely via video and phone, so geography is rarely a barrier. We are happy to travel for key workshops and presentations.

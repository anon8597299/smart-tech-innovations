Four Breathtaking Spaces

Each setting on the estate has its own character and charm. From the intimacy of our Sandstone Chapel to the grandeur of the Heritage Barn, your vision determines the space.

Sandstone Chapel

A beautifully restored 19th-century sandstone chapel with stained glass windows and natural stone walls. The most intimate and romantic setting on the estate, bathed in golden afternoon light.

Up to 80 guests Ceremony only

Heritage Barn

Our flagship reception space. Original timber beams, exposed brick and warm pendant lighting create an atmosphere that is simultaneously rustic and refined. The ultimate Mudgee celebration venue.

Up to 150 guests Ceremony and reception

Garden Terrace

A manicured terraced garden overlooking the vineyard, ideal for outdoor ceremonies and cocktail receptions. Draped in wisteria and surrounded by heritage roses, it is picture perfect year round.

Up to 100 guests Outdoor setting

Luxury Cottages

Six individually styled heritage cottages sleeping two to six guests each. Perfect for the bridal party and family, ensuring your wedding weekend begins and ends on the estate in complete luxury.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do you cater for non-wedding events?

Yes. We host milestone birthdays, corporate retreats, engagement parties and charity events. The estate is available Monday to Thursday at reduced rates for non-wedding events. Please enquire for specific pricing.

Can we bring our own caterer?

We work with a curated list of preferred caterers who know the estate well. Couples are welcome to choose from our preferred list or, in some cases, bring an approved external caterer subject to a venue fee and induction. We are flexible and want your food to be perfect.

What is the accommodation situation?

The estate has six individually styled heritage cottages sleeping between two and six guests each. Package inclusions vary — Classic includes four cottage nights, Grand includes all six cottages for the full weekend. Additional cottage nights can be added at $450 to $680 per night depending on the cottage.

How far in advance should we book?

Spring and autumn Saturday dates typically book 18 to 24 months in advance. Midweek and winter dates are more available. We recommend enquiring as soon as you have a date in mind. We can hold a date for 14 days with a fully refundable deposit while you make your decision.

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

The Heritage Barn and Garden Terrace are fully accessible. The Sandstone Chapel has a step at the entrance and we provide a portable ramp on request. All estate paths between main areas are compacted gravel suitable for wheelchairs. Please let us know your specific requirements when enquiring.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that circumstances change. Date changes are offered complimentary once within 18 months of the original booking. Cancellations more than 12 months out receive a 75% refund of deposit. Cancellations within 12 months are subject to our standard terms, which your coordinator will walk you through at booking.

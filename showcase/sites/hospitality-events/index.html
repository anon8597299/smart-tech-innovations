Our Venue Spaces

Four unique settings, one unforgettable estate

From the quiet reverence of the sandstone chapel at dawn to the warm glow of the heritage barn at midnight — every space at Stonebridge has been shaped by 15 years of wedding days and the love stories that happened within them.

Up to 80 guests

The Sandstone Chapel

Built in the 1890s and lovingly restored, the chapel is Stonebridge's most iconic space. Morning light pours through original leadlight windows onto warm sandstone walls. The bell tower still rings on wedding days.

✓ Ceremony up to 80 guests ✓ Original 1890s architecture ✓ Leadlight windows ✓ PA system installed
Up to 150 guests

The Heritage Barn & Cellar

A century-old timber and iron barn transformed into a breathtaking reception space. Exposed beams draped with fairy lights, a polished concrete dance floor, and our signature barrel-lined cellar room for intimate pre-dinner drinks.

✓ Reception up to 150 guests ✓ Original exposed timber beams ✓ Dance floor & stage ✓ Full in-house catering kitchen
Up to 100 guests

The Garden Terrace

Outdoor ceremonies with the Orange ridgeline as backdrop. The manicured terrace features a rose-framed arbour, flagstone pathways, and established olive trees. When the wisteria blooms in spring, it is truly something else.

✓ Outdoor ceremony up to 100 ✓ Rose & wisteria arbour ✓ Established olive grove ✓ Wet weather backup included

The Cottage Retreat

Six luxury self-contained cottages set among the estate gardens, available exclusively to the wedding party. Stone fireplaces, king beds, claw-foot baths, and private verandahs with vineyard views.

✓ 6 self-contained cottages ✓ Exclusive to wedding party ✓ Stone fireplaces & claw-foot baths ✓ From $380/night (all 6)

In-House Catering

Orange's finest on every plate

Our kitchen team has been cooking for Stonebridge weddings since 2009 — and they take it personally. Every menu starts with what's in season from local Orange producers: stone fruits from the orchards, lamb from the Central West tablelands, vegetables grown within 50 kilometres.

We work with you to create a menu that reflects who you are as a couple. Whether that's a relaxed grazing feast under the olive trees or a formal four-course dinner in the barn, the food will be something your guests talk about for months.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions couples ask us most

We cater exclusively in-house. Our kitchen team has been cooking for Stonebridge weddings since 2009, and in-house catering is one of the reasons our food consistently receives such strong feedback. It means we can guarantee quality, accommodate all dietary requirements, and coordinate timing seamlessly with the rest of your day. We hold a full liquor licence and work with an outstanding selection of local Orange region wines and craft beverages.

Absolutely — and we strongly encourage it. There is something about standing in the chapel or walking through the barn that no photograph can fully capture. Our team hosts guided estate tours Monday to Friday during business hours and by appointment on weekends. Call us on (02) 6372 4488 or use the enquiry form below — we suggest allowing 1.5 hours to see all four spaces properly.

After your site visit and personalised quote, securing your date requires a 20% deposit and a signed venue agreement. The remaining balance is invoiced in two stages — at 6 months prior and 4 weeks prior to your wedding. Peak Saturdays (October through April) book 12–18 months ahead. If you have a date in mind, reach out early even if you're not quite ready to commit — we can do a preliminary availability check.

No — every Stonebridge wedding includes a dedicated on-site coordinator who works with you from the moment you book. They will help with timelines, vendor coordination, ceremony logistics, and everything in between. On your wedding day they are there from setup to the final song. Many of our couples find they do not need an external planner at all, though if you have one already, our coordinators are experienced at working alongside them.

Yes — our six luxury cottages can be booked by any couple marrying at Stonebridge. They are available exclusively to the wedding party; no other guests are on the property on your wedding night. Cottage rates start from $380/night (off-peak) for all six combined, rising to $480/night in peak season. Most couples book for two nights — the night before for the bridal party getting-ready experience, and the wedding night itself.

Every outdoor ceremony booking includes a wet weather backup at no extra cost. The sandstone chapel (up to 80 guests) is available as the backup for garden terrace ceremonies. For larger outdoor events we have a permanent marquee structure that can be deployed. Our coordinator monitors forecasts in the days prior and will have a clear contingency plan ready — you will never be scrambling on the morning.

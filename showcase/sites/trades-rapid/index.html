Full Showcase Site • Trade Style

A complete electrician website style built for calls, quote forms, and local trust.

This demo is a full-site concept, not just a landing page. It shows how page structure, CTA placement, and service clarity combine to increase lead quality for trade businesses.

What this style is optimised for

  • Urgent enquiries with fast contact paths
  • Homeowner trust with clear proof and service scope
  • Local SEO structure for suburb + service intent
  • Simple forms that improve enquiry completion rate

What clients can customise

  • Service categories and priority jobs
  • Visual style and brand colours
  • Lead routing (email/SMS/call)
  • Tracking events for ads and SEO

Why these homepage components matter

Each block below exists because it improves a business metric, not because it “looks good”.

Primary CTA above the fold → Faster action

Visitors who are ready now should not have to scroll to contact. This increases call and form starts.

Service-group tiles → Better self-selection

When users quickly find their exact service, bounce drops and qualified enquiries increase.

Trust strip (licence, response times, reviews) → Lower hesitation

Proof blocks remove uncertainty and reduce comparison shopping exits.

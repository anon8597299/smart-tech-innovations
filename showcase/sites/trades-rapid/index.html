What we do

Our plumbing services

Residential and light commercial plumbing across Bathurst and the Central West. Every job comes with a written fixed-price quote — no surprises on the invoice.

Blocked Drains

CCTV drain inspection and high-pressure water jetting. We find the cause — not just clear the symptom — and give you a permanent fix.

Hot Water Systems

Supply and install gas, electric, solar and heat pump systems. Same-day replacement available. All major brands serviced and warranted.

Bathroom Renovations

Full bathroom and laundry fit-outs from plans to final fixtures. We manage the complete plumbing scope — new builds welcome.

New Construction

Rough-in and fit-out for new builds and major renovations. Builders welcome. Experience with residential and light commercial projects.

General Repairs

Leaking taps, burst pipes, toilet repairs, and all general plumbing maintenance. No job too small. Fixed-price every time, no call-out fee.

Emergency Callouts

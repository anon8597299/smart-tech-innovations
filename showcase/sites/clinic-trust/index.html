Bulk billing available — no out-of-pocket cost

Hillcrest Family Clinic bulk bills eligible patients, meaning your GP consultation is fully covered by Medicare with no gap payment.

Who qualifies for bulk billing?

  • Concession card holders (Pensioner Concession, Health Care Card, CSHC)
  • Children aged 15 and under
  • DVA card holders
  • Patients with a valid Medicare card — eligibility may vary by appointment type

A standard out-of-pocket fee applies for non-eligible patients. Our reception team will always advise you of any costs before your appointment.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a referral to see a GP at Hillcrest?
No — you can book directly with any of our GPs without a referral. A GP referral is required if you want to access our allied health services (physiotherapy, psychology, or dietetics) under a Medicare rebate.
Who is eligible for bulk billing?
Concession card holders (Pensioner Concession, Health Care Card, Commonwealth Seniors Health Card), children aged 15 and under, and DVA card holders are bulk billed. All other patients with a valid Medicare card should ask our reception team at time of booking as eligibility can vary by appointment type.
Can I request a same-day appointment?
Yes. We keep capacity available for urgent same-day appointments. Call us on 02 6362 1234 first thing in the morning for the best chance of a same-day slot. Please advise reception if your matter is urgent.
Do you accept new patients?
Yes, Hillcrest Family Clinic warmly welcomes new patients. Please bring a photo ID and your Medicare card to your first appointment. If you're transferring from another practice, we can request your medical records with your written consent.

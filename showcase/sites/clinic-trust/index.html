Bulk billing available — no out-of-pocket cost

Hillcrest Family Clinic bulk bills eligible patients, meaning your GP consultation is fully covered by Medicare with no gap payment.

Who qualifies for bulk billing?

  • Concession card holders (Pensioner Concession Card, Health Care Card, Commonwealth Seniors Health Card)
  • Children aged 15 and under
  • DVA card holders
  • Patients with a valid Medicare card — ask reception at booking, as eligibility may vary by appointment type

A standard out-of-pocket fee applies for non-eligible patients. Our reception team will always advise you of any costs before your appointment.

Before Your Visit

What to bring to your appointment

To help us provide the best possible care, please bring the following items to your appointment:

  • Medicare card — required for bulk billing and to claim rebates on services.
  • Referrals and specialist letters — if you're seeing a new GP or following up specialist advice, bring any existing letters or reports.
  • Current medication list — bring all medications or a written list, including any vitamins and supplements you take.
  • Concession card — Pensioner Concession, Health Care Card, or DVA card if applicable.
  • Previous test results — blood tests, x-rays, or scan results if you have them.
  • Private health insurance card — for any applicable rebates on allied health services.

New to Hillcrest?

We warmly welcome new patients. Our friendly reception team will register you on your first visit. Bring a photo ID along with your Medicare card.

If you're transferring from another practice, we can request your medical records with your written consent.

Allied Health

On-site allied health — Medicare rebates available

Our co-located allied health team works alongside our GPs to provide integrated, coordinated care. A GP referral may entitle you to Medicare-subsidised sessions.

Physiotherapy

Our qualified physiotherapists treat sports injuries, back and neck pain, post-surgical rehabilitation, and movement disorders. Initial and ongoing appointments available.

Medicare Rebate: Up to 5 sessions per calendar year under a Chronic Disease Management plan (GP referral required).
  • Sports injuries & sprains
  • Back, neck & joint pain
  • Post-surgical rehabilitation
  • Workplace injury recovery

Psychology

Evidence-based psychological therapy for anxiety, depression, trauma, and relationship difficulties. Our registered psychologists provide a safe, non-judgmental space.

Medicare Rebate: Up to 10 individual sessions per calendar year under a GP Mental Health Care Plan (MHCP).
  • Anxiety & depression
  • Trauma & PTSD
  • Stress & burnout
  • Grief & adjustment

Dietetics

Personalised nutrition assessment and therapy for diabetes, heart disease, weight management, digestive conditions, and eating disorder recovery.

Medicare Rebate: Up to 5 sessions per calendar year under a Chronic Disease Management plan (GP referral required).
  • Diabetes & pre-diabetes
  • Heart disease & cholesterol
  • Weight management
  • Gut health & IBS

Private health insurance rebates may also apply. Check with your insurer. To access Medicare-rebated sessions, start with a GP appointment at Hillcrest — we'll coordinate your entire care plan.

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

How does fee-for-service advice differ from commission-based advice?
With fee-for-service advice, you pay us directly for our time and expertise — just like you pay a solicitor or accountant. We receive no commissions from product providers. Every recommendation is because it's right for you, not because it pays us better.
Should I set up an SMSF?
An SMSF makes sense for some clients and not others. Generally they become cost-effective with a combined balance above $250,000–$350,000, and suit people who want direct control over investments including property. We'll give you an honest assessment based on your balance, goals and willingness to take on trustee responsibilities.
When should I start retirement planning?
The earlier, the better — but starting at 50 is still very valuable. The 10–15 years before retirement are typically the highest-earning years and most powerful for super contributions. If you're within 5 years of retirement, specific tax and pension strategies must be planned well in advance.
Do you work with clients outside Dubbo?
Yes. We work with clients across Central West NSW including Orange, Bathurst, Parkes, Forbes, Mudgee and surrounds. Most ongoing meetings are via video call for clients outside Dubbo, with in-office visits for initial sessions and annual reviews.

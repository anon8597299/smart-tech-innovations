Solutions Architecture

Service page structure that pre-qualifies leads before they ever call.

This page demonstrates how a solar site can separate residential, commercial, and battery buyers while explaining exactly why each section improves sales efficiency.

Segmented service tiles → Faster pathway selection

Distinct pathways for homes, businesses, and battery upgrades reduce browsing confusion and lift conversion into the correct enquiry flow.

Outcome comparison table → Better-fit customer expectations

Showing payback period ranges and system outcomes helps set realistic expectations, reducing wasted consultations and churn risk.

Finance and rebate section → Less sales friction

Clear upfront financing and rebate context means fewer objections late in the sales process and shorter close cycles.

FAQ intent blocks → SEO + conversion win

Answering common intent questions (roof type, battery value, blackout backup) supports rankings while reducing drop-off from uncertainty.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How much does a solar system cost in Orange?
A typical residential system (6.6kW) costs $5,500–$8,000 after the STC rebate. The rebate is deducted from your invoice by SunVolt — you never have to claim it yourself. System size and cost vary based on your roof, usage and inverter choice. Your free assessment includes a detailed written quote.
What does CEC accredited mean?
The Clean Energy Council (CEC) accredits solar installers who have passed industry training and adhere to the Australian Standard for solar installations. CEC accreditation is required to access government STC rebates. All SunVolt installers are CEC accredited — not just the company.
How long does installation take?
Most standard residential jobs are completed in a single day. Grid connection (via Essential Energy) typically takes 3–5 business days after installation. From accepting a quote to your system being fully operational is generally 10–14 business days.
Do you use subcontractors?
Never. Every SunVolt installation is done by our own CEC accredited team. No subcontractors, ever. This means you can hold us accountable for the quality of your install for the full 10-year workmanship warranty period — you know exactly who put your system on.

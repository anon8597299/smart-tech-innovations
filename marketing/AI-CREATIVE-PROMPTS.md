# AI Creative Prompt Pack (Sora / Image Models)

Use this pack to generate high-quality visuals for the ImproveYourSite showcase ecosystem.

## Global Constraints

- Australia-first visual context (subtle cues, realistic homes/streets/shops)
- No brand logos, no embedded text, no watermarks
- Realistic lighting, clean composition, mobile crop-safe framing
- Keep hero focus centered so it survives 9:16, 1:1, and 16:9 crops

---

## 1) TradesRapid (Electrician)

### Hero image prompt

> Photorealistic Australian electrician in a clean modern home, installing a switchboard with safety gear, warm morning light, trustworthy professional mood, premium but practical, shallow depth of field, no logos, no text.

### Short Sora clip prompt (6-8s)

> Cinematic but realistic 8-second clip of a licensed electrician arriving at a suburban Australian home, greeting homeowner, then working carefully on an electrical panel. Warm natural daylight, handheld-stable movement, clean professional uniform without logos, no text on screen.

### Component value use

- Trust strip background
- Hero section visual proof
- About page credibility cutaway

## Suggested Rendering Specs

- **Still hero images:** 1536x1024 (plus cropped 1024x1536 variant)
- **Video snippets:** 1080x1920 + 1920x1080 exports
- **Web output:** WebP (quality high), AVIF optional

## QA Checklist

- Face/hands look natural
- No weird tool anatomy or unrealistic objects
- No accidental brand marks/text
- Safe mobile crop on subject
- Scene matches the page purpose (trust, clarity, conversion)

- Business cards: `utm_source=business_card&utm_medium=print&utm_campaign=money_leak_scan`

## 6) Production checklist

- [ ] Print QR at minimum 25mm x 25mm
- [ ] Test scan from 1m distance
- [ ] Confirm mobile load under 2 seconds
- [ ] Route leads to inbox/CRM
- [ ] Track source in every lead submission
- [ ] Review lead source weekly and scale top channel

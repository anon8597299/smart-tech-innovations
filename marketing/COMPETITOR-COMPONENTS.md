# Competitor-Inspired Component Upgrade (2026-02-17)

Objective: compare high-performing agency sites and apply interaction-heavy components to improve engagement + lead capture.

## Research snapshots

- Conversion-focused structure and repeated CTAs in first screen depth
  - https://www.tilipmandigital.com/resource-center/articles/high-converting-websites
- Sticky CTA and persistent action patterns
  - https://webflow.com/blog/cta-examples
  - https://conversion-rate-experts.com/sticky-cta-win-report/
- Social proof + testimonial-first persuasion
  - https://www.webfx.com/blog/web-design/examples-of-websites-that-convert/

## Components adopted into ImproveYourSite

1. **Sticky CTA bar**
   - Always-available conversion action after user scroll depth.
   - Message: *"Your website is costing you money. Get the free audit."*

2. **Testimonials interaction block**
   - Horizontal scroll cards with next/prev controls.
   - Increases trust and keeps users moving down-funnel.

3. **Revenue Leak ROI Calculator**
   - Interactive estimator to convert passive visitors into active leads.
   - Calculates potential monthly and annual revenue uplift.

4. **Offline QR campaign section**
   - Campaign-ready message + QR tracking model for coasters/cards/radio tie-ins.

## Expected impact

- Higher scroll depth and engagement time
- Stronger trust via social proof
- More qualified inquiries via calculator intent
- Better attribution from offline channels

## Patterns worth emulating (without copying)

1. **Proof-first trust architecture**
   - Awards/accreditations visible in the primary flow
   - Strong review positioning and testimonial depth
   - Clear long-term confidence messaging (guarantee/risk-reversal framing)

2. **Repeated conversion prompts**
   - Multiple strategically placed CTAs
   - Calculator/estimate action repeated across page depth

3. **Community + outcomes narrative**
   - Not just services; strong transformation story + customer outcomes
   - Clear practical benefit statements tied to savings/growth

4. **Local relevance at scale**
   - Strong geo relevance in content and structure
   - Useful model for rolling out suburb/city clusters

Common questions

Do I need any technical skills?
No technical skills needed. We walk you through the API key setup personally — exactly where to go and what to copy. After that, Jarvis runs itself. If you can check your email, you can run Jarvis.
What is OpenClaw and Claude?
Jarvis is powered by two AI tools: OpenClaw (the agent orchestration engine that runs all 10 of your agents) and Claude by Anthropic (the AI brain that reads, writes and decides). We use these same tools to run our own business. You get a Claude API key from Anthropic — they bill you directly for usage (typically $10–30/month). We don't touch your billing.
What are my options for getting Jarvis?
Four options: Digital Download ($250) — install on your existing Mac or Windows computer. USB Drive ($250) — pre-loaded USB shipped to you, plug in and run. Business Manager Pro ($3,000) — a Mac Mini with Jarvis fully pre-installed and shipped anywhere in Australia. Managed Plan ($199/month) — we run everything for you, including Jarvis, your website, and content.
What happens if my computer is turned off?
Jarvis pauses when your computer is off and resumes automatically when you log back in. For 24/7 operation, the Business Manager Pro (Mac Mini) is designed to stay on at the office — or leave your existing computer running. Either way, Jarvis picks up exactly where it left off.
Is my business data safe?
Yes. Everything runs on your own hardware — your emails, lead data, and business information never leave your machine. The only external call is to the Anthropic API (Claude) to process AI tasks. You own your hardware, you own your data.
Can I connect it to my existing email or Instagram?
Yes. We walk you through connecting Jarvis to your Gmail inbox and Instagram business account as part of the setup. Both take under 5 minutes. For Business Manager Pro customers, we handle this remotely for you.
What industries does it work for?
Jarvis works best for service businesses: trades (plumbers, electricians, builders), healthcare (clinics, physio, dental), finance (accountants, brokers), hospitality, and retail. Any Australian business that gets leads via email and promotes on Instagram will see immediate results.

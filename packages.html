Common questions

What happens after I pay?

You're redirected to a short form to fill in your business details ‚Äî name, services, colours, and template preference. We confirm receipt within 1 business day and start work immediately.

What's the difference between Scan & Fix and Complete Build?

Scan & Fix improves your current website. We fix performance, SEO, and conversion issues without rebuilding. Complete Build is a brand-new site designed from scratch to your brand.

What does the media team do for Premium Growth?

We coordinate professional photography and videography sessions at your business, giving you fresh, authentic imagery to use across your site, social media, and ads.

Do I need to supply content?

For Complete Build and Premium Growth, we write the content based on your business details form. For Scan & Fix, we work with your existing copy and improve it where needed.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes, if you start with Scan & Fix and want a full rebuild later, we apply your previous payment as credit. Just email us when you're ready to upgrade.

What if I'm not happy?

We do revision rounds to make sure you love the outcome. Scan & Fix includes 1 revision, Complete Build includes 3, and Premium Growth includes unlimited revisions within 30 days.
